# Docaz — Project Context

## Concept

Tele-expertise service for second opinions on endometriosis.

Connects patients with specialist doctors for remote second opinions, focused exclusively on endometriosis diagnosis, treatment plans, and surgical recommendations.

## Problem

Endometriosis affects ~1 in 10 women and takes an average of 7–10 years to diagnose. Patients often lack access to specialists, struggle to get second opinions, and navigate a fragmented medical system with limited information.

## Solution

A platform where patients can:
- Submit their medical history, imaging, and reports
- Get matched with endometriosis specialists
- Receive a structured second opinion asynchronously (tele-expertise)

## Target Users

- **Patients**: women diagnosed or suspected of having endometriosis
- **Doctors**: gynecologists and surgeons specializing in endometriosis
